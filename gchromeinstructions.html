This application is not in the Chrome App Store. Therefore you will have to enable Developer

Mode in Google Chrome. 

 

 

1- Download the Extension by clicking here. 

 

2- Extract the AzureTool.Zip file somewhere on your computer. 

 

3- Open chrome://extensions/ in Google Chrome and click on "Developer mode" on the top right.

 

4- Click on "Load Unpacked" 

 

5- Select the unzipped AzureTool folder. 

 

6- The application is now installed. (do not move or delete the unzipped AzureTool folder)
